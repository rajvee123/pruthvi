About Us

We are a non-profit NGO

Prithvi Zero Waste Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to creating a sustainable and plastic-free future for our planet. Founded in 2010, our mission is to raise awareness about the detrimental effects of plastic waste and promote sustainable waste management practices.
